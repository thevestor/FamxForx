Founded in Shenzhen, China, year 1995, Senyang Electronic Science & Technology (Shenzhen) Co., Ltd. is a

large scale electronics enterprise that specializes in the research, development and marketing of electronic products.

In 2008, we formally ventured into the field of mobile phones and the trademark of “CITYCALL” obtained a

superior quality reputation and brought great effect to the market. With the development of technology and

economy, 2012 we also develop tablet to meet the social demand growth. 2013 our industrial park was built,

has 36000㎡floor areas.

Since it was established, we based on the business theory “Quality, Trust and Customers First “ and our company have

kept on introducing advanced management methods. Furthermore, we persist to keep improving and seek excellence.

As a high-standard producer, we have state of the art manufacturing plant, including dustless workshops, clean and

comfortable surroundings. Based on the products of our progressive machineries and perfect quality assurance system,

our company is accredited by ISO 9001:2008 for Quality Management Systems, ISO14001:2004 for Environmental

Management Systems and GB/T28001-2001 for Occupational Health and Safety Management Systems. in addition,

we also got the CE Certification which means our products meet the European standards.

With over 1000 staffs, we have formed a complete team of very competitive engineers, technologists and quality controllers

and established long-time collaborative relationships with leading companies and manufactures. We developed high quality

and modern technology communication products based on innovation. Sales are over 6 million units every year with

numbers kinds of mobile phones successfully exported to Africa, South America, India, Middle East, Southeast Asia and the

UAE markets. With dedication, our vision is to grow larger that ever.

Looking forward with a new image, power in innovation and global orientation, CITYCALL will take the lead in conquering the future.
